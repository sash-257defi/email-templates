Dear Potential Investor,
 

Thank you for subscribing to our ICO mailing list. We’re very pleased to inform you that the token sale will take place from 15 November 2017 to 25 December 2017.

Here's a brief recap of why we started this ICO journey and what you can expect:

Why Binary.com is performing an ICO
Binary.com was founded in 1999. Over the years, we've grown to become a well-established and very successful company. The next step for a company of our size is naturally to go public through an IPO. However, we believe that ICOs will gradually replace IPOs in the coming decade, thanks to the numerous benefits of blockchain technology.

Benefits of an IPO on the blockchain
ICOs and cryptocurrency exchanges allow us to go beyond the limitations of traditional IPOs and stock exchanges. Investors can look forward to 24/7 trading, faster settlement times, lower trading costs, as well as an immutable and public audit trail. Funding your cryptocurrency exchange account with Bitcoin or Ether is also cheaper, easier, and faster.

How the token sale works
Binary.com will issue up to 10 million ERC20 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. Potential investors can purchase these tokens through an open auction on our website starting 15 November. Our tokens come with three main benefits. Firstly, the tokens grant you the right to receive dividend-linked payments. Secondly, you have the right to convert your tokens into Ordinary Shares. Thirdly, you can freely trade your tokens on the Lykke.com cryptoasset exchange after the ICO, allowing the tokens to potentially rise in value -- similar to shares on a stock exchange.

For a better understanding of the token sale, please read our Information Memorandum. It provides a full disclosure of our financial history and risk factors that may affect our profitability.

We look forward to ushering in the future of IPOs and a new era of blockchain innovation with you on board.


Regards,


Binary.com
